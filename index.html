Gym Management System

Bachelor of Computer Application Project Report

Project Synopsis

The software to be produced is a "Gym Management System" with two main users: Admin and Receptionist (Gym Instructor). Receptionists can manage member details, attendance, and equipment usage. Admins have higher authority, including user account management and database updates.

Objectives and Scope

Objectives

  • Provide a system to handle gym member information and attendance efficiently.
  • Maintain records of gym equipment and medicines used by members.

Scope

This system automates gym records management, including equipment, medicines, and attendance tracking. It provides an efficient way to manage gym operations and ensures smooth functionality with reduced effort and cost.

System Features

  • Admin and Receptionist roles with respective authorities.
  • Attendance tracking and member information storage.
  • Equipment and medicine database management.
  • Comprehensive reporting and analytics for gym operations.
